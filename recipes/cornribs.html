Ingredients

  • Sweet corns
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder/Grated garlic

Steps

  1. To Prepare the seasoning In a bowl, take Olive Oil 2 Tbsp Then add Paprika, Black pepper powder, salt & Grated Garlic/Garlic powder - 1 tsp. If you dont have paprika use red chilli powder
  2. To cut the sweet corn ribs Cut the sweet corn vertically in 4 parts. Tips to cut the corn ribs:

    3. Tips to cut the corn ribs

    • Cut on a steady surface
    • Use a Sharp Knife
    • Pat on the knife with heavy weight Cutting the corns can be dangerous, so if you are not confident use the whole corn.
  3. To prepare corn ribs Apply the prepared seasoning on the corn rib using a brush. If you donâ€™t have a brush you can apply using a spoon or directly apply with your hands.
  4. To bake the Corn Ribs in the Oven Pre-heat the oven for 5 mins at 375 F or 190 C Then Take a Baking Tray Cover with a parchment paper or butter paper Place the corn ribs Place the tray in the oven Bake for approx 30 mins at 375 F or 190 C or until the corn gets curled and slightly charred
